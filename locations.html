Districts of Shroudkeep

The city climbs from the factory wards of the Hems through Old Town and finally to Silk Street, where wealth rises above the smoke it creates.

Industrial ward

The Hems

Textile mills, plague wards, worker tenements, soot-black canals, and near-constant Sentinel patrols.

Plague: Severe
Magic Risk: Extreme
Control: Sentinels and factory owners

Hostile

Historic ward

Old Town

Abandoned churches, narrow alleys, old tunnels, crowded lodging houses, and pockets of organized resistance.

Plague: Moderate
Magic Risk: High
Control: Contested

Contested

Upper ward

Silk Street

Private clubs, vampire estates, immaculate avenues, gaslight, and violence concealed behind servants' doors.

Plague: Officially none
Magic Risk: Extreme
Control: Four Families
