What the City Admits

These are not secret dossiers. They represent common knowledge, public rumor, and the sort of warnings a citizen learns by surviving long enough. Names and private loyalties will be added after the party encounters them.

Municipal enforcement

The Sentinels

Black-coated officers empowered to enforce curfew, suppress magic, seize contraband, and conduct arrests without ordinary warrants. Their masks are designed to be remembered.

Avoid scrutiny

Old power

The Founding Houses

Four ancient families whose estates, industries, and charitable institutions shape the city. Their rivalry is polite in print and considerably less polite elsewhere.

Untouchable

Blackglass Gaol

The Gaolers

Wardens, clerks, guards, and physicians assigned to the prison. Some are cruel. Some are frightened. In Shroudkeep, those qualities often share a uniform.

Immediate threat

Public health authority

The Plague Doctors

Licensed physicians identifiable by their dark coats, sealed cases, and masks. They may quarantine a household, condemn a building, or remove a body without family consent.

Authority varies

Industrial ownership

The Factory Masters

Owners, foremen, and agents who control wages, housing, debt, and access to medicine throughout the Hems. Most workers know better than to complain where a clerk can hear them.

Powerful

Official classification

You

Prisoners awaiting judgment. Your papers are incomplete, your charges may be false, and the city has taken an unusual interest in ensuring you never reach an ordinary courtroom.
