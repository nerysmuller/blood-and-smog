A City Built in Layers

Shroudkeep is inspired by the dense, irregular growth of late-Victorian London: old streets buried beneath newer roads, industrial corridors carved through poorer wards, bridges controlled as checkpoints, and wealth climbing physically away from the smoke. The map below is a period visual reference rather than a literal street-for-street plan.

Desolamus

Desolamus draws its silhouette and regional mood from historic Moldavia: river-cut borders, wooded interiors, old roads, mountain approaches, and settlements separated by long stretches of uneasy country.

Session One knowledge

Shroudkeep is the capital and the center of industrial power. Most citizens know the country beyond it chiefly through freight routes, military reports, family stories, and rumor.
